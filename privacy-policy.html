Overview

1. Information We Collect

HomeOptima collects personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register for an account, use our services, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and property preferences. We are committed to collecting only the data necessary to deliver our services.

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our platform, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This information helps us understand how our platform is used and enables us to improve the user experience for all visitors.

Cookies and similar tracking technologies may be used to collect and store information about your preferences and interactions with our platform. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, and to communicate with you about your account and our offerings. Your data helps us personalize your experience and deliver property recommendations and market insights relevant to your needs.

  • To create and manage your HomeOptima account.
  • To send transactional emails such as account confirmations and password resets.
  • To deliver personalized property listings and market updates.
  • To analyze platform usage and improve our features and content.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

HomeOptima does not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform, conducting our business, or serving you, provided that those parties agree to keep this information confidential and use it only for the purposes we specify.

We may disclose your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, and you will be notified in advance.

4. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. You may also object to or restrict certain types of processing, or request a portable copy of your data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

If you have subscribed to marketing communications, you may opt out at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly. Please note that even after opting out of marketing emails, we may still send you transactional messages related to your account or services you have requested.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your personal data is handled, please contact our privacy team. We are dedicated to addressing your inquiries promptly and transparently.

You can reach us by email at contact@homeoptima.ca or by phone at (123) 456-7896. Our team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM EST.
